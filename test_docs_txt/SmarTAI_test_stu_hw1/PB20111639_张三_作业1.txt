## 3.

**Proof:**
$G$ is solvable, suppose $H \leqslant G$, $N \unlhd G$
$$
1=G_0 \unlhd G_1 \unlhd \ldots \unlhd G_s=G
$$

Consider $H_i=G_i \cap H \quad i=0,1 \ldots, s$
obviously $H_i \leqslant H_{i+1} \quad i=0,1 \ldots, s-1$
$\forall g \in H_{i+1}, \forall x \in H_i$
$g x g^{-1} \in H$ because $g, x \in H$
$g x g^{-1} \in G_i$ because $G_i \unlhd G_{i+1}$
So $H_i \unlhd H_{i+1} \quad i=0,1, \cdots ,s-1$
$$
\begin{aligned}
H_{i+1} / H_i & =\left(G_{i+1} \cap H\right) /\left(G_i \cap H\right) \\
& =\left(G_{i+1} \cap H\right) G_i / G_i \\
& \leq G_{i+1} / G_i
\end{aligned}
$$

$\Rightarrow H_{i+1} / H_i$ is abelian

$$
1=H_0 \unlhd H_1 \unlhd \cdots \unlhd H_s=G \cap H=H
$$

so $H$ is solvable.

Consider $\quad \bar{G}_i=G_i N / N \quad i=0,1 \cdots s$ 

obviously $\quad \overline{G_i} \leqslant \overline{G_{i+1}}$
$$
\overline{G_{i+1}} / \overline{G_i} \cong G_{i+1} N / G_i N
$$

$G_{i+1} / G_i$ is abelian $\Rightarrow \overline{G_{i+1}} / \overline{G_i}$ is abelian

$$
1=\bar{G}_0 \unlhd \bar{G}_1 \unlhd \cdots \unlhd \bar{G}_s=G N / N=G / N
$$

so $G / N$ is solvable.

## 4.

以轴线为轴、 $r$ 为半径，在硫酸铜溶液中取一个厚为 $\mathrm{d}r$，长为 $l$ 的圆筒，这圆筒的电阻为
$$
\mathrm{d}R=\rho\ \frac{\mathrm{d}r}{2\pi r l}=\frac{\rho}{2\pi l} \frac{\mathrm{d}r}{r}
$$
积分便得
$$
R=\frac{\rho}{2\pi l} \int_a^b \frac{\mathrm{d}r}{r}=\frac{\rho}{2\pi l} \ln{\frac{b}{a}}
$$
设两筒的电势差为U，内筒上的电荷量为Q，则由对称性和高斯定理得硫酸铜溶液中的电场强度为
$$
E=\frac{\lambda}{2\pi\varepsilon}\frac{\textbf{r}}{r^2}=\frac{Q}{2\pi\varepsilon l}\frac{\textbf{r}}{r^2}
$$
式中$\lambda$ 是内筒单位长度的电荷量， $\textbf{r}$ 是从轴线到场点的位矢，于是得
$$
U=\int_a^b \textbf{E} \cdot \mathrm{d}\textbf{r}=\frac{Q}{2\pi\varepsilon l}\int_a^b\frac{\textbf{r}\cdot \mathrm{d}\textbf{r}}{r^2}=\frac{Q}{2\pi\varepsilon l}\ln{\frac{b}{a}}
$$
所以电容 $C=\frac{Q}{U}=\frac{2\pi \varepsilon l}{\ln(b/a)}$
