$$
\int_0^1\ x\ \arctan{x}\ \mathrm{d}x\\
=\left.\arctan{x}\frac{1}{2} x^2 \right|_{x=0}^{x=1}-\frac{1}{2}\int_0^1\ x^2 \mathrm{d}(\arctan{x})\\
=\frac{\pi}{8}-\frac{1}{2}\int_0^1\ \frac{x^2}{1+x^2} \mathrm{d}x\\
=\frac{\pi}{8}-\frac{1}{2} +\frac{1}{2}\int_0^1\ \frac{1}{1+x^2} \mathrm{d}x\\
=\frac{\pi}{8}-\frac{1}{2}+\left. \frac{1}{2}\arctan{x}\right|_{x=0}^{x=1}\\
=\frac{\pi}{4}-\frac{1}{2}
$$
